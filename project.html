My Open World Game

A 3D space exploration and colonization game similar to space engineers and kerbal space program.

This is still an ongoing project!

Goal

Make a 3D space exploration and colonization game similar to space engineers and kerbal space program.

Implementation

Engine: Unity
IDE: Visual Studio

  • C#
  • HLSL
Algorithms and Data Structures:
  • Marching cubes Isosurface construction
  • Sparse octree based terrain meshing
  • Simplex noise based procedural terrain generation.

Result

  • Can procedurally generate planets >100km in diameter with reasonable framerates.
  • World saving, loading, and streaming
  • Floating origin allowing the player to travel massive distances
  • Title and load screens
